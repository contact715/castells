# Project Brief: Castells

## 1. Client Overview
**Client Name:** Castells
**Industry:** Digital Marketing / Growth Agency
**Tagline:** "We are the Anti-Agency."
**Mission:** To stop clients from burning money on vanity metrics and focus entirely on revenue and ROAS. They position themselves as a "Growth Department" rather than a vendor.

## 2. Project Goals
- **Primary Goal:** Generate high-quality leads (audits) from businesses looking to scale.
- **Secondary Goal:** Establish authority and trust through a premium, data-driven brand aesthetic.
- **Key Message:** "Precision First. Velocity. Partnership."
- **Differentiation:** Data-driven, revenue-focused, anti-vanity metrics, high-end aesthetic ("Classic serif elegance with contemporary minimalism").

## 3. Target Audience
- **Primary:** Founders, CEOs, and CMOs of scaling businesses (E-commerce, SaaS, Professional Services, Local Business).
- **Needs:** Rapid revenue growth, improved ROAS, transparent reporting, strategic partnership.
- **Pain Points:** Wasted ad spend, lack of clear results, slow agency response times, generic strategies.

## 4. Key Features & Requirements
- **Interactive Growth Audit:** A multi-step form to qualify leads and gather data.
- **Case Studies:** Detailed breakdowns of past successes with concrete metrics (Revenue, ROAS, etc.).
- **Premium Visuals:** High-end animations (Aurora, Light Rays), smooth scrolling, and interactive elements to convey sophistication.
- **Content Hub:** A blog/insights section to demonstrate expertise.
- **Trust Signals:** Partner logos, testimonials, and team bios.

## 5. Design Aesthetic
- **Style:** Minimalist, high-end, editorial.
- **Colors:** Ivory/Black/White base with Coral (#E08576) accents.
- **Typography:** Serif headings (Playfair Display/similar) for elegance, Sans-serif body for clarity.
- **Vibe:** "The Anti-Agency" - serious, effective, exclusive.
